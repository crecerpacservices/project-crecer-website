Privacy Policy

Technical Data Security & Privacy Standards

At Crecer, we understand that engineering and construction projects involve sensitive technical data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle the information you provide during inquiries and project planning.

1. Data Collection

We collect information necessary to provide accurate technical proposals and service execution, including:

  • Contact details (Name, Work Email, Phone Number).
  • Project specifics (Site location, technical descriptions, service type).
  • Project attachments (Site photos, preliminary drawings, or sketches).

2. Use of Information

Information provided is strictly used for:

  • Generating Bills of Quantities (BOQ) and technical proposals.
  • Communication regarding project timelines and site inspections.
  • Operational coordination of the core engineering team.

3. Data Confidentiality

We do not sell, trade, or leak project data to third parties. Access to technical drawings and site information is restricted to the core leadership and project staff involved in the specific engagement.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard administrative and technical measures to protect your inquiry data. Digital submissions are processed via secure channels (Formspree) with end-to-end encryption for attachments.

5. Your Rights

Clients may request the deletion of their project inquiry data from our active ledger once the coordination or engagement has concluded.
