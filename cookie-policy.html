Cookie Policy

Website Performance & Analytics Cookies

This policy explains how we use cookies to provide a smooth, high-performance experience on the Crecer website.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser that help our website recognize your device and remember specific preferences for future visits.

2. Essential Cookies

We use functional cookies to ensure critical site features work correctly, including:

  • Navigation: Maintaining your path across our multi-page portfolio.
  • Security: Protecting our contact form from automated spam and bot activity.
  • Performance: Ensuring that styles and fonts load at the speed required for a premium experience.

3. Local Storage

We may use browser local storage to save temporary site preferences, ensuring you don't have to re-read introductory information on every visit.

4. Managing Your Preferences

Users can adjust their browser settings to block or delete cookies. However, please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the technical functionality of our inquiry forms.
